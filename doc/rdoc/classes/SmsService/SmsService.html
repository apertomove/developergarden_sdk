Public Instance methods

send_flash_sms(numbers, sms_message, originator, environment = 2, account = "")

Send a flasg sms. A flash sms goes directly to the handy screen. Detailed information about sending of sms can be found under:

  • www.developergarden.com/openapi/dokumentation/services#4.2.3.

Parameters

numbers:Up to 10 receivers can be specified separated by commas (",").
sms_message:Actual message. Can be up to 765 characters. A sms will be charged for each 153 chars.
originator:String to be displayed as the originator of the message.

Max. 11 characters. Further chars will be cut of. Allowed chars are [a-zA-Z0-9]. At least one letter needs to be present.

environment:Service environment as defined in ServiceEnvironment.
account:Currently unused
